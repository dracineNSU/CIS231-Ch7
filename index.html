About Us

Here at the Cajun Foods Franchises we serve a lot of things from the Cajun Cusinie. We sell all the items needed to serve the following dishes and MORE.

  • Crawfish/shrimp Etouffee
  • Crawfish/shrimp boil
  • Jambalaya
  • Gumbo
  • Boudin/boudin balls

Each store has a manager, who keeps track of the inventory, and handles the day-to-day operations.

Also, if you need something for another Cajun dish that we do NOT serve, we can ship it to our store in less than 24 hours.
